About Us

Founded in 2024, the Crypto Country Club unites connoisseurs of blockchain technology, top-tier investors, and forward-thinking innovators. Our mission: to provide an opulent environment where members can network, learn, and celebrate the evolving world of cryptocurrency in true style.

Rules

  • Membership Minimum: A minimum of 1 million in market cap is required to join and remain in the club.
  • Sell Rule: Members must sell 10% of their holdings per each additional million in market cap. Rule violations may result in immediate dismissal from the club.
  • Confidentiality: All transactions and personal data remain strictly private.
  • Dress Code: Adherence to black-tie and business formal attire in all public club areas.
  • Professional Conduct: Respect, courtesy, and decorum are expected at all times.
  • Access Control: Vaults and private lounges require membership badges for entry.
  • Event RSVP: Attendance at exclusive events requires advance registration.
